## added
+ Given a stock ID in cell `A2` of the Google SpreadSheet, get stock price of the stock ID.

## How to 
To get stock price,
1. enter a stock ID in cell `A2` of the Google SpreadSheet.
2. select `Custom menu` -> `fetch data` -> `fetch stock price`
